13eta ── WHERE PEOPLE CONNECT

LinkedIn is a great way to network and extend connections in regards to your career, but what if you want to connect with other students, perhaps in your classes or major?

13eta is an academic and social networking app for UVA students in order to provide a better way for students to connect and study collaboratively. It draws inspiration from Tinder and LinkedIn by using similar matching and networking aspects. Below is a quick tutorial with illustrations on how to use the application.


Demo:

Previous Next

  • Be sure to sign in with your UVA email ID.
  • Create/Edit your profile by clicking on the "User Profile" dropdown menu and continuing to "My Profile".
  • Head over to the "Find a Match" tab to view students with similar interests and skills.
  • You can also search for profiles by name, by major, or by interests using the search bar in the top right.
  • Match with other users, get in contact, and get studying!
